ABOUT

  • Welcome to my personal website! I am a healthcare professional with over 20 years of experience in rehabilitation and dialysis.

  • My core strength as a healthcare professional lies in my ability to create and communicate a shared vision that motivates and inspires teams to achieve their goals.

  • As a healthcare professional, I hold several certificates that have helped me refine my skills and stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends.

What makes me unique is my belief that the right people are the key to success in any workplace. I am passionate about bringing people of different backgrounds together to accomplish greater outcomes for the vulnerable to chronically ill that normally would not have been possible. My values of integrity, persistence, and empathy are at the core of everything I do.

I have experience leading innovation amongst the most challenging and highly regulated industries within healthcare.
